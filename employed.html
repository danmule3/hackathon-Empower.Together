Where to Get Employed

Explore different platforms and methods to find job opportunities that suit your skills and interests.

Job Search Websites

  • LinkedIn Jobs

    A professional networking site that offers job listings across various industries. You can also connect with recruiters and network with other professionals.

  • Indeed

    A comprehensive job search engine that aggregates job postings from various sources. Indeed offers options for filtering jobs by location, salary, and job type.

  • Remote.co

    Dedicated to remote job opportunities across multiple fields, including tech, marketing, and customer service.

  • FlexJobs

    Focuses on remote, part-time, freelance, and flexible job opportunities, ensuring that all listings are hand-screened for legitimacy.

  • SimplyHired

    A job search engine that provides access to millions of jobs and offers salary estimations, company reviews, and job alerts.

  • AngelList

    A platform for startup jobs where you can find roles in innovative companies, particularly in tech. You can apply directly to startups and see their funding details.

Professional Associations and Organizations

  • American Psychological Association (APA)

    Offers job boards, career resources, and networking events for professionals in psychology and related fields.

  • National Law Review Job Board

    A resource for legal professionals providing job listings specifically in the legal field, along with articles and insights.

Tips for Job Search

Consider enhancing your profile on professional networking sites, tailoring your resume for specific jobs, and practicing your interview skills.
